# Objective!

FEM-Onramp is a place where we expose our learnings in FEM and the solutions to the proplems we faced. Simple and awesome, made with ❤.

# How can you contribute?

Simple, forward us the shameful email to derive.science@gmail.com where it’ll be up for moderation. We kindly ask you to censor any personal information from the email before forwarding it to us. Once your submission is accepted by our team it’ll be published on the site (opens new window).

# How it does work?

Once a submission is approved, a commit is pushed in this GitHub repository (opens new window). We then use Forestry (opens new window) to make content editing and publishing easier for our awesome folks 🙌.
